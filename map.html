Khotan

Due to mudslides triggered by prolonged monsoons in 2022, the typically arid Brew Village faced significant adversity, resulting in the destruction of numerous houses and displacement of families. In response to this crisis, our team visited the affected area to assess the damage and develop an environmentally sustainable rehabilitation strategy by designing a structure using local materials and techniques. The aforementioned project demonstrated cost-efficiency by utilizing locally sourced materials.
